Spectral Imaging with QUBIC: Component separation methods using Bolometric Interferometry
This presentation introduces QUBIC, a bolometric interferometer designed to detect CMB B-modes by combining high-sensitivity bolometers with interferometric control of systematics. It introduces two spectral-imaging–based component separation methods, Frequency Map-Making (FMM) and Component Map-Making (CMM), which exploit the instrument’s intrinsic spectral resolution to enhance foreground mitigation and improve cosmological constraints.
